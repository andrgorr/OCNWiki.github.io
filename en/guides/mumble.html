Download

In order to get started, grab Mumble from their website. Pick whichever relates to your machine.

Connecting

When you get mumble installed you will need to connect to a server. You can connect to Overcasts server by entering the adress/IP which is mumble.oc.tc. Keep the port default. Use the same username as your IGN

Settings

Hop over to the configure tab in the top left of your Mumble window.

  • Click the box that says Advanced.
  • You should be in Audio Input, the device should be the microphone you are using to communicate, and disable the echo feature. While in there it would be great to set up push to talk.
  • Then click Audio Output. Change your device to whatever you're listening to the sound from, most likely if you're using a headset, it should be the same as the device for the audio input.
  • After output, click user interface. Choose whichever layout you prefer. Feel free to mess around with that.
  • After that, click on shortcuts. This is where push to talk should come into play, if you don't see a shortcut for push to talk, click add. Click unassigned and change it to push to talk. Then click the empty area and hit a key that you would like to use for the button that will activate your microphone. If you have multiple buttons on your mouse you should try settings it there as its a really good spot for it.
