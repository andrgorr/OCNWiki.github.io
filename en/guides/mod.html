Do you want to become a moderator on the Overcast Network? This awesome guide will show you how.


You will be invited to become a mod if the admins think you're good enough. So don't even think about asking.

There are several things that will help your chances of becoming a mod.
1. Be helpful to other players.
2. Talk with other players, mods and admins. (But don't annoy them.)
3. Have a positive attitude and proper etiquette.
4. Report rule breakers. (Don't make up fake reports.)
Doing these things will not guarantee in any way, shape or form that you will become a mod.

Things that will not help you in becoming a mod.
1. Do not ask for mod.
2. Telling us how trustworthy you are.
3. Saying how good you will be as a mod.
4. Intentionally breaking the rules.
5. Having a bad attitude towards other players.

Note That:
Mods are not more important than the players, because without players we would not need mods.

Being a mod isn't really a promotion. This is because once you are a mod you will no longer be seen as a player but more as a babysitter. A babysitter that has to watch ~99 screaming, mostly annoying kids. This is why patience is useful to be a good mod.

You will not receive any perks for being a mod, the only thing you will receive is the ability to be a volunteer babysitter.

FAQ:


May I ask if you are still seeking for mods?
Yes we are always actively looking for new mods, however we will choose them.

I have noticed that some mods have become inactive like CustardInc, and I would like his/her place to be transferred to us non-mods.
Mod positions are not transferable, we will not take away an inactive mods position and give it to a player.

How will I be invited to become a mod.
You may be invited privately in-game, by skype, mumble, etc depending what is convenient, and what you use.

Fine, but I'll still ask to become a mod.
Well that's your choice, however these requests will simply be ignored or hidden.
